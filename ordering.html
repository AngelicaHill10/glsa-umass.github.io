How to Order

There are two formats available for purchase:

1. Hardcopy:
More recent editions are available as hardcopies offered on Amazon and Createspace. To purchase a hardcopy, find the volume in the publications tab, and follow the link to one of our vendors.
2. PDF:

Go to the Publications webpages and select the volumes you would like to purchase. You can then pay by VISA or Mastercard using PayPal. Once your purchase has been confirmed, a PDF copy of the volume will be emailed to you by the GLSA.

Methods of Payment

We accept and recommend Credit Card payment for PDFs. We accept both VISA and Mastercard. Please note: we do not accept payment by debit card, American Express, or Discover Card. Hardcopies may be purchased on Amazon as usual.
