Notes on searching

The Type, Field,and Status options are related by logical OR.

For example, to search for books that are either about Psycholinguistics or Phonetics or both, check the Psycholinguistics and Phonetics boxes and no others.

Similarly, to search for books that are either Available or Out of Print, check both Status boxes.  To search for books that are only Out of Print, check the Out of Print box and leave the Available box unchecked.
