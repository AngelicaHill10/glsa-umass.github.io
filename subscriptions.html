Subscriptions

Individuals, institutions, and libraries may subscribe to GLSA.

Subscribing has the following advantages:

  • A 10% discount.
  • Publications are shipped very soon after being printed.
  • Other discounts are available on special publications and CD-ROMs.

Subscriptions are tailored to your needs.  You may choose whether to have standing orders for any or all of the following:

  • UMOPs
  • NELS proceedings
  • Dissertations.
How to Subscribe

There are two ways to subscribe:

  • Either fill out the on-line subscription form, or
  • Download, fill in, and print out the subscription form [Text] [PDF]. Then either mail, fax, or e-mail it as an attachment to GLSA.
